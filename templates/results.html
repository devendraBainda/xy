Understanding the classification results

Lung Benign Tissue

Normal lung tissue without signs of cancer. Characterized by regular cellular structure and organization.

Lung Squamous Cell Carcinoma

A type of non-small cell lung cancer that begins in the squamous cells of the lung. Often associated with smoking.

Lung Adenocarcinoma

A type of non-small cell lung cancer that begins in the glandular cells of the lung. The most common type of lung cancer.
